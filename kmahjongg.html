KMahjongg Help

This program is a clone of the well known mahjongg game for the KDE Desktop.

Your mission in this game is to remove all tiles from the game board. A matching pair of tiles can be removed, if they are 'free', which means that no others tiles block them on the left or right side.

What is not yet implemented:

  • Online help
  • Loading and saving of games
  • bugfixes
This game requires a 256 (or more) color display on a minimum resolution of 640 x 480.

It was not tested on different hardware so far.

I hope you will enjoy this game, please feel free to send suggestions or bug-reports to Mathias Mueller .
